About

Hey! I'm Zach Barnard — a freelance web and graphic designer currently based in Memphis, TN. I got my start making event fliers and shirt designs for myself and friends in the diy music scene. Through my previous work with local non-profits, I further developed these skills making fliers and info-graphics for community outreach campaigns. Along the way, I discovered a real passion for design and the creative problem-solving that comes with coding.
Now, I help small businesses and individuals bring their ideas to life through thoughtful design and functional, clean web experiences. If you're looking for a designer who can bring some personality and purpose to your project, let's chat — I'd love to hear what you're working on!
